Program has to detect humans in first place 
and then gender, age and posture 
1. Deep neural nets convolutional neural network for Living Being Classification
2. Random Forest algorithm  for Posture Detection 
3. Detect Age of Human and Gender also 

Centroid Tracker 
This framework aims to develop a simple and efficient object tracking and detection using yolo
